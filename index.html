About Bahubali Statue

The Bahubali Statue in Karkala is a monumental Jain sculpture dedicated to Bahubali, a revered figure in Jainism celebrated for his commitment to non-violence and renunciation. Standing approximately 42 feet tall and carved from a single granite block, the statue epitomizes the triumph of spiritual awakening over worldly attachments.

Crafted with exquisite detail, the statue captures the serene and stoic expression of Bahubali, inviting reflection and reverence. It is not only an important religious symbol but also a remarkable piece of ancient artistry that has become a landmark in Karkala. The site is enriched by its historical context and scenic surroundings, which include ancient temples and tranquil landscapes.
